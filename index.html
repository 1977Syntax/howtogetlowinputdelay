Why It Matters

Input delay can impact your gaming performance. Lowering it can give you a competitive edge!

Steps to Reduce Input Delay

  1. Optimize Game Settings:

    Lower your in-game graphics settings.

  2. Enable Low Latency Modes:

    Utilize NVIDIA or AMD low latency settings.

  3. Use Fullscreen Mode:

    Switch to fullscreen for better performance.

  4. Adjust Polling Rate:

    Set your mouse polling rate to 1000Hz.

  5. Update Drivers:

    Keep your GPU and system drivers updated.

  6. Reduce Background Processes:

    Close unnecessary programs running in the background.

  7. Wired Internet Connection:

    Use an Ethernet connection for stability.

Key Facts

  • Input delay is influenced by hardware and software.
  • Lowering graphics settings can increase FPS.
  • Polling rate: Higher rates lead to faster response times.
  • Low latency modes optimize GPU processing.
  • Fullscreen mode can reduce input lag.
