Optimize Your Fortnite Experience

Hardware Recommendations

To achieve the best performance and reduce input delay, consider the following hardware options:

  • Wired Connection: Use an Ethernet cable to connect your gaming device directly to the router. This minimizes latency and ensures a stable connection.
  • High Refresh Rate Monitor: Choose a monitor with a refresh rate of at least 144Hz for smoother visuals and quicker response times.
  • Gaming Mouse: Select a gaming mouse with low response times (1 ms or less) for enhanced precision.
  • Mechanical Keyboards: These often provide faster response times and better tactile feedback.

Wi-Fi Settings

If a wired connection isn't possible, optimize your Wi-Fi settings:

  • Router Placement: Place your router in a central location to reduce interference.
  • Frequency Band: Use the 5GHz band for faster speeds and reduced latency compared to 2.4GHz.
  • Reduce Interference: Keep other electronic devices away from your router to minimize interference.
  • Quality of Service (QoS): Enable QoS settings on your router to prioritize gaming traffic over other network activities.
