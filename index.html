Use Ethernet Cable

Using an Ethernet cable can significantly improve your connection stability and reduce lag, resulting in a smoother gaming experience.

Optimize Settings

Adjust your graphics settings for better performance. Lower settings can lead to higher frame rates.

Close Background Applications

Make sure to close any unnecessary applications running in the background to free up resources.
